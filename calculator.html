💡 Which is the smarter funding source? Here is what the numbers say: 🔴 EPF / Akaun Sejahtera earns approximately ~6% dividend per year. This is higher than most home loan rates (~4% p.a.). Money left in EPF earns more than what you save by reducing your loan. Keep EPF intact if you have another option.

🟢 Own Savings / Fixed Deposit earns only about ~3.5–3.8% p.a. — which is lower than your mortgage rate (~4%). Savings sitting idle in FD cost you money while your loan accrues interest at a higher rate. Use savings for the down payment first.

📌 Recommended strategy: Use savings/FD first → take the highest loan margin your bank approves → keep EPF compounding at ~6%. Only withdraw EPF if savings are genuinely insufficient.
